FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Osprey AI.

What is AI orchestration?

AI orchestration is the process of coordinating multiple AI agents, models, and workflows to work together seamlessly. Osprey AI provides the infrastructure to build, deploy, and manage these complex systems at enterprise scale.

How secure is my data?

Your data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.3. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and HIPAA ready. All data is stored in secure, compliance-certified datacenters with 24/7 monitoring.

Can I use my own AI models?

Yes! Osprey AI supports custom models and fine-tuned models. You can bring your own models from Hugging Face, deploy custom APIs, or use our model training service to create specialized agents.

What kind of support do you offer?

Free plans include community support. Professional plans get priority support with 4-hour SLA. Enterprise customers get dedicated support engineers with 1-hour SLA, Slack integration, and on-call support.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is based on the number of API calls and features you need. Start free with up to 10K calls/month. Professional plans start at $299/month. Enterprise pricing is custom based on your usage.

Can I deploy on-premise?

Yes, enterprise customers can deploy Osprey AI on-premise or in their own cloud environment (AWS, Azure, GCP). Contact our sales team to discuss deployment options.
